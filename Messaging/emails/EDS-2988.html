You’ll lose BT Featureline benefits when you switch
Without BT Featureline, you’ll no longer have network features including Call Divert, Call Waiting and 3 Way Calling. You also won’t be able to transfer calls for free or make free calls within your BT Featureline group of numbers.

We’ll also need to change your BT Featureline line back to a standard telephone line. This costs £30 for each line. You’ll see these charges on your bill.

How other products and services will be affected
Switching your line to a new provider will affect some BT products – but not others.
By switching broadband too, your BT Business Broadband service will end:

•You’ll lose your BT email address and BT Wi-Fi account
•You’ll lose your 0560 numbers used for VoIP calls (over broadband via your hub)
